Tx Pulse 600-036

By Enless Wireless

The Enless Wireless Tx Pulse 600-036 consists of a pulse sensor that is compatible with pulse meters with a dry contact interface or an open collector inlet. It transmits pulse counts over the LoRaWAN® network. Tx Pulse 600-036 will quickly and economically transform your conventional gas meter into a smart meter.
